Privacy Policy for SunTimer

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

SunTimer ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Data

SunTimer requires access to your device's location to provide accurate UV index information and personalized sun exposure recommendations. This location data is:

  • Used only to fetch real-time UV index data for your current location
  • Not stored on our servers
  • Not shared with third parties
  • Processed locally on your device

2.2 Personal Information

We collect and store locally on your device:

  • Your skin type selection (for personalized sun exposure calculations)
  • App preferences and settings
  • Sun exposure history and timer data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide real-time UV index information based on your location
  • Calculate personalized sun exposure times based on your skin type
  • Send notifications to help you avoid sun overexposure
  • Track your sun exposure sessions
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

All personal data (skin type, preferences, exposure history) is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms. We do not maintain servers that store your personal information.

5. Third-Party Services

SunTimer uses third-party services to provide UV index data. These services may have their own privacy policies:

  • UV index data providers (for real-time UV information)
  • Location services (provided by your device's operating system)

6. Permissions

SunTimer requires the following permissions:

  • Location: To fetch UV index data for your current location
  • Notifications: To alert you about sun exposure limits
  • Vibration: To provide haptic feedback for alerts

7. Children's Privacy

SunTimer is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored locally on your device
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data
  • Disable location permissions (though this will limit app functionality)
  • Opt out of notifications in your device settings

9. Data Retention

Your data is stored locally on your device and remains there until you:

  • Uninstall the app
  • Clear the app's data through device settings
  • Manually delete data within the app (if applicable)

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
